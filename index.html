FAQs

How much does a website cost?

Our packages start from Rp 59k for basic tasks up to Custom pricing for complex projects. We tailor everything to your specific budget, especially for students!

Do you offer source files?

Yes, Pro and Business plans include all editable source files (Figma, After Effects, Illustrator) so you retain full control.

What is the turnaround time?

Speed is our forte. Usually 1-2 weeks depending on complexity. Basic landing pages can be done in 3 days.

Can you handle rush orders?

Absolutely. We offer an 'Express Lane' service for an additional fee if you need deliverables within 24-48 hours.

Do you provide hosting?

We can set up hosting for you on Vercel or Netlify, giving you a fast, secure, and free start for most static sites.
